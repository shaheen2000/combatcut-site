THE PROTOCOL

Your fight week, mapped out

CombatCut builds a personalized day-by-day plan from the moment you set your fight date — so you're never guessing what today's number should be.

01
7 days outBaseline
Log your starting weight and confirm your target class. CombatCut calculates the total cut needed and builds your daily targets backward from weigh-in.
02
6–3 days outWater & sodium
Gradual water loading and sodium adjustments, tracked daily. The app flags if you're ahead or behind plan so you can adjust before it becomes a problem.
03
2 days outDepletion
Carb and water taper begins. Your readout updates in real time as you log each check-in, with a clear lbs-to-go counter.
04
Weigh-in dayFinal cut & rehydration
Step on the scale knowing your number. The moment you're cleared, CombatCut switches to your rehydration and refeed plan for fight time.
